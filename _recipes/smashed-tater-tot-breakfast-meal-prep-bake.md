notes: >
  A crispy smashed tater tot crust layered with sausage, tomatoes, chives,
  cheddar, and eggs. Great for make-ahead breakfasts. Reheat in an air fryer
  for the best texture.
ingredients:
  - Potato Crust:
    - 2 lb frozen tater tots, thawed
    - 2 tbsp melted butter, optional
  - Potato Seasoning Blend:
    - 1 tbsp dried garlic chips
    - 1 tbsp dried minced onion
    - 2 tsp smoked paprika
    - 2 tsp kosher salt
    - 1 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
  - Filling:
    - 1 lb breakfast sausage, cooked and well drained
    - 1 14.5 oz can diced tomatoes, thoroughly drained
    - 2 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
    - 2 cups shredded sharp cheddar cheese, divided
  - Egg Mixture:
    - 8 large eggs
    - 2 tbsp milk or heavy cream, optional
    - 1/2 tsp kosher salt
    - 1/2 tsp freshly cracked black pepper
    - 1 tbsp fresh chives, chopped
directions:
  - Potato Crust:
    - Preheat the oven to 425°F. Lightly grease a 9x13-inch baking dish or individual oven-safe dishes.
    - Using a spice grinder or mortar and pestle, hand-mill the dried garlic chips, dried minced onion, smoked paprika, salt, and black pepper into a coarse seasoning blend.
    - Spread the thawed tater tots evenly into the prepared baking dish. Smash them firmly into a single even layer using the bottom of a glass or measuring cup.
    - Brush the potato layer lightly with melted butter, if using, and season generously with the potato seasoning blend.
    - Bake for 25 to 30 minutes, or until the potato crust is golden brown and crisp around the edges.
  - Filling:
    - Remove the crisp potato crust from the oven and sprinkle with about 1/2 cup of the cheddar cheese.
    - Evenly layer the cooked breakfast sausage, drained diced tomatoes, chopped chives, and remaining cheddar cheese over the crust.
  - Egg Mixture:
    - In a large bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk or cream if using, salt, pepper, and chopped chives until smooth.
    - Pour the egg mixture evenly over the filling.
  - Bake:
    - Return the dish to the oven and bake for 20 to 25 minutes, or until the eggs are fully set and the cheese is melted and lightly browned.
    - Let the casserole rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving.
    - For meal prep, cool completely before portioning into containers and refrigerating.
  - Meal Prep Tips:
    - Drain the diced tomatoes thoroughly to prevent excess moisture.
    - Brown and drain the breakfast sausage well before assembling.
    - Always bake the potato crust until crisp before adding the filling.
    - Cool completely before covering and refrigerating.
    - Reheat in an air fryer at 350°F for 5 to 8 minutes for the best texture and a crispy potato crust.
