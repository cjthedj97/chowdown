ingredients:
- 1 1/2 lbs flank steak, thinly sliced
- 1/4 cup soy sauce
- 2 tbsp brown sugar
- 1 tbsp sesame oil
- 1 tbsp rice vinegar
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
- 1 tbsp gochujang
- 1/2 tsp black pepper
- 8 small flour tortillas
- 2 cups shredded cabbage
- 1/4 cup chopped cilantro
- 2 green onions, sliced
- 1 lime, cut into wedges
- 1/2 cup spicy mayo or sriracha mayo

directions:
- In a bowl, whisk soy sauce, brown sugar, sesame oil, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, gochujang, and black pepper.
- Add sliced steak and toss to coat. Marinate for at least 15 minutes.
- Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and cook the steak in batches for 2 to 3 minutes per batch until browned.
- Warm tortillas in a dry skillet or microwave until pliable.
- Build tacos with cabbage, bulgogi beef, cilantro, and green onions.
- Drizzle with spicy mayo and finish with fresh lime juice.
---

If you have time, marinate the beef for an hour for deeper flavor. Quick-pickled onions are also great on top.
